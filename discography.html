The Original Playlists

The "Nedery and Mecera" album was created by consolidating three conceptual playlists, each representing a different chapter in the Navigator's journey. The full tracklists are archived here.

Playlist: The First Hum

What is the sound of the moment before the beginning? Not silence, but a chord of "nearly" infinite possibility, waiting to be struck. This is the sound of that ocean.

  • The Unheard Chord

Playlist: The Navigator's Sail

Are we merely passengers on the great river of time, carried along by its currents? Or can we learn to be its navigators? This is the story of how a passenger becomes a navigator.

  • The Cosmic River
  • Glimpses Of The Deep
  • The Cosmic Tide
  • Hoist The Soul

Playlist: The Observer's Gaze

What happens in the silence after the roar is gone? This is a collection of songs about the consequence of action, the burden of choice, and the responsibility that comes with being a composer of your own reality.

  • The Weight Of The Helm
  • The Ghost Current
  • To Read The Tides
  • Lanterns In The Deep
  • The Cosmic Observer Gaze
